ABOUT ME

I'm a retired professional cyclist that spent most of my teens-twenties traveling the US and beyond. While competing on an international level, my niche of cycling came with injuries and they did come aplenty. One thing I learned about myself while rehabbing and getting healthy was that I had a deep interest in creating websites—websites for anything I may have been interested in at the time. But, it wasn’t until recently that I decided that this was something I wanted to do for a career.
